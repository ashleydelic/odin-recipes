Chakalaka

A proudly South African dish: Chakalaka

Ask any South African "what is chakalaka?", and watch their eyes glow. This is a mouth-watering spicy vegetable relish that is best served with bread, stews, meat and more.

Chakalaka is often described as a street food, as it can be enjoyed while on the move. Traditionally served with pap, easilymakes this dish an end of the day meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oil, or as needed
  • 3 medium onions, diced
  • 2 medium green bell peppers, diced
  • 3 medium carrots, diced
  • 3 medium tomatoes, diced
  • Â¼ cup water
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

    • Cooking instructions

    1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat; stir in onions and bell peppers. Cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes.
    2. Add carrots, tomatoes, water, curry powder, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until mixture is well combined and thickened, 15 to 20 minutes.
